Flappy bird copy with toolbar
Qt(C++) project

Project from scratch. This project shows my fundamental knowledge of Qt library and C++ language.

Application appearance and bird movements are close to original game. Score counter is located in the upper left corner of the screen. Toolbar at the top of the scene allows to switch bird or game field. There are 3 birds with different look and movement style. There are 2 game fields: classic Flappy bird and Extreme. Extreme is faster. Press on pictures to see gameplay.













I didn't follow any tutorial to create this project. I used the knowledge I gained during my studies. You can download and see the code.
